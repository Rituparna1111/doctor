About Me

Dr Debasree Gangopadhyay is a practicing Pediatric Cardiologist with over 10 years of clinical experience in the field of Pediatric Cardiology. She has been trained in both India and abroad in the field of Pediatric cardiology. She is an expert in various childhood cardiac interventions and fetal echocardiography. She is actively involved in teaching and training and clinical research.

SPECIALIZATION

Fetal Cardiology

Fetal echocardiography is a test similar to an ultrasound.The exam uses sound waves that “echo” off the structures of the fetus’s heart.

Know more

Interventional Cardiology

Interventional cardiology is a non-surgical option which uses a catheter – a small, flexible tube – to repair damaged or weakened vessels, narrowed arteries, or other affected parts of the heart structure.

Know More

Non-Invasive Cardiology

Non-invasive cardiology identifies heart problems without using any needles, fluids, or other instruments which are inserted into the body.
